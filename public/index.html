App Installation

Install QRMeet on your device's home screen for quick access during the event without losing your tab.

To install QRMeet on your iPhone or iPad, tap the Share icon below, then select Add to Home Screen.

Privacy

What is stored on your device — A pseudonymous game identifier, an authentication token, your chosen display name and emoji, and a temporary QR token are saved in your browser's local storage (not cookies). Tap "Reset game" below to erase them.

What is stored on our servers — Your pseudonymous identifier, chosen display name, emoji, room membership, and encounter history (who met whom and when) are stored in a Cloudflare database for the lifetime of the room — by default one week from its creation — after which they are automatically and permanently deleted. The exact deletion time is shown as a countdown on the room's board and admin pages.

IP address — Your IP address is used transiently to prevent abuse (rate-limiting); it is not stored beyond 1 hour and is not linked to your game profile.

Cloudflare — This app runs on Cloudflare Workers, CDN, and Analytics. Cloudflare processes request metadata (IP, user-agent, country) as a data processor and may collect aggregated, anonymous traffic statistics. Cloudflare may set one or more functional cookies (e.g. for bot protection or security challenges); these are strictly necessary for the service to operate and are not used for tracking. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

No third-party trackers. No advertising, analytics SDK, or social-media pixel is embedded in this app.
